# No CS degree, are you at a disadvantage in cloud, and how do you close it?

Recruiters may lean toward candidates with a Computer Science or Computer Engineering degree. But without one, you can build up your profile: ship as many projects as you can, create technical documentation and content, learn as many in-demand tools as possible, and deliberately develop your problem-solving skills.

A degree signals ability; a portfolio proves it, and proof usually wins.
