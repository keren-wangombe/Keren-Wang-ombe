# Beyond certs, what really gets you an entry-level cloud role?

The skills that land an entry-level cloud role are almost inexhaustible, but beyond AWS certification and knowledge, recruiters look for both hard and soft skills: the ability to build and document real projects, familiarity with in-demand tools, communication, and above all problem-solving.

Certifications open the door; problem-solving and communication are what keep you in the room.
